TASK: For the wizards in each deposit group, show the longest magic wand.

Order the result by longest magic wand for each deposit group in ascending order, then by deposit_group alphabetically.

Choose a fitting name for the new column.


SELECT `deposit_group`,MAX(`magic_wand_size`) AS 'longest_magic_wand'
FROM `wizard_deposits`
GROUP BY `deposit_group`
ORDER BY `longest_magic_wand`, `deposit_group`;
