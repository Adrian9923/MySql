TASK: Write a query that returns all unique first letters of wizardsâ€™ first names, but only if they have made a deposit of type Troll Chest.

Order them alphabetically.

Use GROUP BY to ensure the uniqueness of the entries.



SELECT LEFT(`first_name`,1) AS 'first_letter'
FROM `wizard_deposits`
WHERE `deposit_group` = 'Troll Chest'
GROUP BY `first_letter`
ORDER BY `first_letter`;
