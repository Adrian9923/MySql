TASK: Write an SQL query that finds the first names of all employees that are both:

In the departments with IDs of 3 or 10

Hired between 1995 and 2005 (inclusively)

Order the information by id.



SELECT `first_name`
FROM `employees`
WHERE `department_id` IN(3,10) AND hire_date BETWEEN '1995-01-01' AND '2005-12-31'
ORDER BY `employee_id`;
