TASK:Extract from the database those photos that their upload day is 10 and summarize their description.

**The summary must be 30 symbols long plus "..." at the end. **

Order the results by date descending order.

Required Columns

"summary" (address)

"date"






SELECT CONCAT(LEFT(p.`description`, 30), '', '...') AS 'summary', p.`date`
FROM `photos` AS p
WHERE DAY(p.`date`) = 10
ORDER BY p.`date` DESC;
