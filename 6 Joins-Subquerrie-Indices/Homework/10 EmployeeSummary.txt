TASK: Write a query that selects the following fields:

employee_id

employee_name

manager_name

department_name

Show the first 5 employees (only for employees who have a manager) with their managers and the departments they are in (show the departments of the employees).

Order by employee_id.


SELECT e.`employee_id`, CONCAT_WS(' ',  e.`first_name`, e.`last_name`) AS 'employee_name'
, d.`name` AS 'department_name'
FROM `employees` AS e
JOIN `departments` AS d
ON e.`department_id` = d.`department_id`
ORDER BY e.`employee_id`
LIMIT 5;
