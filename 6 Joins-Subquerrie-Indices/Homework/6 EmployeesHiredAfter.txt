TASK:
Write a query that selects the following fields:

first_name

last_name

hire_date

dept_name

Filter only the employees that were hired after 1/1/1999 and work either in the "Sales" or the "Finance" departments.

Sort the result by hire_date in ascending order.

SELECT e.`first_name`, e.`last_name`,
 e.`hire_date`, d.`name` AS 'dept_name'
FROM `employees` AS e
JOIN `departments` AS d
ON e.`department_id` = d.`department_id`
WHERE e.`hire_date` > '1999-01-01' AND d.`department_id` IN(3, 10)
ORDER BY e.`hire_date`;







V2:

SELECT e.`first_name`, e.`last_name`,
DATE_FORMAT(e.`hire_date`, '%Y-%m-%d %H:%i:%s') AS 'hire_date', d.`name` AS 'dept_name'
FROM `employees` AS e
JOIN `departments` AS d
ON e.`department_id` = d.`department_id`
WHERE e.`hire_date` > '1999-01-01' AND d.`department_id` IN(3, 10)
ORDER BY e.`hire_date`;
