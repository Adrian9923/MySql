TASK: Use SoftUni Database for this problem.

Write a query that finds the following information for all employees who do not have a manager:

employee_id

first_name

last_name

department_id

salary



SELECT e.`employee_id`, e.`first_name`, e.`last_name`, d.`department_id`, e.`salary`
FROM `employees` AS e
JOIN `departments` AS d
ON e.`department_id` = d.`department_id`
WHERE e.`manager_id` IS NULL;

SAU
