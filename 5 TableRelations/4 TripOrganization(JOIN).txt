TASK: Use the database that is called "Camp Database".

Write a query that retrieves information about SoftUni Camp's transport organization.

It should get information about the drivers (name and id) and their vehicle_type.





SELECT v.`driver_id`,v.`vehicle_type`
,CONCAT_WS(' ', c.`first_name`, c.`last_name`) AS 'driver_name'
FROM `vehicles` AS v
JOIN `campers` AS c
ON c.`id` = v.`driver_id`;
