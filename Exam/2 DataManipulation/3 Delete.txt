TASK: Some of the clients have not used the services of our company recently, so we need to remove them from the database.

Delete all clients from the clients table that do not have any courses and the count of the characters in the full_name field is more than 3 characters.

CHAT GPT DAR NU e BUN
DELETE FROM `clients`
WHERE `id` NOT IN (
    SELECT c.`id`
    FROM `clients` AS c
    LEFT JOIN `courses` AS co ON c.`id` = co.`client_id`
    GROUP BY c.`id`
    HAVING COUNT(co.`client_id`) > 0
)
AND CHAR_LENGTH(`full_name`) > 3;
