TASK: After many kilometers over the years, the condition of cars is expected to deteriorate.

Update the cars and set their condition to be equal to 'C' if they have a mileage greater than 800000 (inclusive) or NULL and were made before 2010(inclusive).

Skip the cars that contain a make value of Mercedes-Benz.

They can work for many more years.


UPDATE `cars`
SET `condition` = 'C'
WHERE (mileage >= 800000 OR mileage IS NULL) AND year <= 2010 AND make <> 'Mercedes-Benz';
